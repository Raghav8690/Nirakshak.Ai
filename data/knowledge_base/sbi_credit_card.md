# SBI Cashback Credit Card

## Overview
The SBI Cashback Credit Card is designed to reward your everyday spending, particularly online shopping, with direct cashback credited to your account.

## Key Features
* **5% Cashback on Online Spends**: Earn high cashback on all your online purchases, regardless of the merchant.
* **1% Cashback on Offline Spends**: Get rewarded for your in-store purchases as well.
* **No Merchant Restrictions**: Cashback applies across a wide range of categories (with some exclusions like utility bills and rent).
* **Auto-Credit of Cashback**: Cashback is automatically adjusted against your next month's statement.
* **Annual Fee Waiver**: The annual fee of ₹499 is reversed on an annual spend of ₹1 Lakh or more.

## Additional Benefits
* **Fuel Surcharge Waiver**: 1% fuel surcharge waiver across all petrol pumps in India.
* **Contactless Advantage**: Fast and secure payments for everyday small purchases.

## Ideal For
Customers who spend a significant portion of their income (e.g., >40%) on shopping and lifestyle categories.
