# SBI Arogya Premier Health Insurance

## Overview
SBI Arogya Premier Policy is a comprehensive health insurance plan designed for high-net-worth individuals, offering wider coverage and high sum insured options.

## Key Features
* **Sum Insured Options**: ₹10 Lakhs, ₹15 Lakhs, ₹20 Lakhs, and ₹30 Lakhs.
* **No Pre-Policy Medical Test**: Up to 55 years of age for people with no medical history.
* **Cashless Hospitalization**: At over 10,000+ network hospitals across India.
* **Maternity Expenses Covered**: After a waiting period of 9 months.
* **Air Ambulance Cover**: Up to ₹1,000,000.
* **Cumulative Bonus**: Increase in sum insured for every claim-free year.

## Why Choose Arogya Premier?
Medical emergencies can occur at any time, potentially wiping out years of savings. A robust health insurance policy like Arogya Premier ensures you receive the best medical care without worrying about the financial impact. 

## Pricing
Premiums start from as low as ₹500/month for young individuals, making it an affordable safeguard for your future.
