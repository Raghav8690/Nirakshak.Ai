# SBI Mutual Fund - Equity SIP

## Overview
Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) in SBI Equity Mutual Funds is one of the most effective ways to build wealth over the long term. It allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly.

## Key Benefits
* **Rupee Cost Averaging**: Mitigate market volatility by buying more units when prices are low and fewer when high.
* **Power of Compounding**: Reinvesting your returns helps your wealth grow exponentially over time.
* **Affordability**: Start investing with as little as ₹500 per month.
* **Disciplined Investing**: Automate your investments to enforce financial discipline.
* **Professional Management**: Managed by experienced fund managers at SBI Funds Management Ltd.

## Ideal For
* Salaried individuals who recently received a salary hike.
* Investors looking for long-term wealth creation.
* Those who want to beat inflation with equity-linked returns.

## Tax Benefits
Investments in ELSS (Equity Linked Savings Scheme) qualify for tax deduction under Section 80C up to ₹1.5 Lakhs per financial year.
